GAME PROJECTS

Here is my collection of the games I had the pleasure to work on during my degree! Some were developed with custom engines in C++, and others with well-known engines such as Unity and Unreal.

Welcome to my journey in game development! I'd be happy to tell you how my learning process has been during these years and the personal achievements I've gotten.

My starting point was Unity. With the making of a simple platformer, I already discovered what I felt comfortable with during development. I started feeling curious about every mechanic in the game, excited about trying out new things, paying attention to every single detail, and dipping my toes into design. In the next project, I was in charge of designing and programming all the enemies, and I had so much fun doing so. I could already tell what appealed to me the most at that moment.

Next, we developed our first game in a big project with programmers and artists. As a designer, I made sure that communication was on point among the two departments and coordinated the work while successfully programming a very challenging player controller for our fighting game.

With experience, I started taking better care of design, which I portrayed in the racing game we developed. I designed and implemented the player controller and the levels, started to develop my own methods, and was able to put into practice everything I had learned.

The biggest challenge was the biggest project I had worked on yet. It felt like the final boss and the greatest opportunity to show what my team and I were capable of. I discovered how complex design can be and learned about so many considerations and ideas that I am eager to keep exploring in future projects.

Also, during these past years, I have participated in several game jams and won some prizes. I love how these events teach you so many things about both technical and team skills in such short periods of time.
